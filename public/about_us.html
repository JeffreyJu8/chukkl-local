Our Mission

As an entertainment platform, Chukkl is dedicated to curating exceptional entertainment by fostering a Creator First mindset, where both prominent and emerging digital creators collaborate to offer fresh and diverse contents. By doing so, we aim to not only feature content of established creators, but also to greatly empower smaller creators instead of them having to rely on traditional algorithms. Furthermore, Unlike traditional platforms that rely heavily on ads, Chukkl embraces seamless brand partnerships with companies like DraftKings and Peloton, integrating products into content without disrupting the viewer experience. Our platform is not intended to replace YouTube but to serve as a specialized hub for comedy, enhancing discovery and engagement through curated channels, exclusive content, and strategic licensing of shows and movies. We aim to create a comedy-loving universe where creators and fans alike can thrive.

Laugh. It's Free!

Chukkl is a comedic entertainment hub on a mission to champion creators of all sizes and craft nonstop entertainment for fans.

University of Southern California alumni Nick Zier and Jeffrey Ju are entrepreneurs with a shared passion for comedy and the creator economy. Drawing from their diverse experiences in digital media, e-commerce, and innovation, Nick and Jeffrey built a streaming platform tailored for the digital creator community. Their goal is to provide comedians and entertainers with a dedicated platform for showcasing their events, merchandise, and content, enriching the comedy-loving community's experience.
