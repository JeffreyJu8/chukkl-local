At Chukkl, we aspire to be the Comedy Central of the contemporary creator economy.

Our goal is to tackle algorithmic, discovery, and brand safety issues within the creator economy. By centering on the niche of comedy and broadcasting across 24/7 channels, Chukkl amplifies the visibility and exposure for established + emerging talent. From a fan's standpoint, we streamline search efforts and uncover a catalog of videos featuring their favorite creators, potentially revealing uploads they have not yet viewed.

Chukkl is founded by Nick Zier and Jeffrey Ju, alumni of the University of Southern California, and entrepreneurs with backgrounds in digital media, e-commerce, and innovation. Nick's additional experience in writing and producing for comedic creators and brands, along with Jeffrey's technical and operational expertise, led them to identify the opportunity of empowering creators within a curated environment. On a daily basis, Nick spearheads content curation and development while Jeffrey expands its tech stack and improves operations.

Comedy enthusiasts can effortlessly unwind on Chukkl, eliminating the hassle of needing to search for something to watch. Chukkl provides uninterrupted streaming in order to ensure a continuous flow of entertainment. Moreover, it keeps fans informed with timely announcements regarding apparel, upcoming live and virtual shows, sweepstakes and giveaways, and more.

Nick and Jeffrey plan on continuing to improve the platform for their growing audience. Further developments include: cast pages, multiple viewing options, captions, and user profiles.

As they look forward to playing a part in the strategic growth of digital creators and bringing endless entertainment to existing fans + newcomers, they welcome you to join in the laughter and chukkls.
